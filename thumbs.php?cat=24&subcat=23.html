Willo the Wisp

In 1987, Nick spargo, Bobbie's father  created "Willo the Wisp". It was shown on the BBC in 1981 to over 10 million viewers and gathered a cult following.

Bobbie took a decade out of Illustration to work in Animation (see filmograpy) culminating in the creation of the second series of "Willo the Wisp" made for Disney Playhouse.

It took 5 years to bring Willo back to life.  Disney Playhouse broadcast the 26 x 5 minute episodes in 2004 for 3 years.

The images shown are all due to the fine work of many artists (Thank you Elephant Productions, Animoon Ltd and Toonz Animation India.) The images are up on this site for fun and interest.
